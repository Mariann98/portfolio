Gift from afar

My role: UX & UI Designer
Project type: Bespoke Design course

Gift from afar is one of my projects for the University course Bespoke Design. The purpose of this was to design a digital gift-giving experience for people living far away from friends and family tuned to the personality, interests, and needs of a specific user.

Problem statement

Foreign college students like myself are separated from their loved ones and this can be hard especially during special occasions, like birthdays. I want to create a fun digital gift-giving experience to help the giver and receiver build a deeper connection with each other and make the distance between them easier.

User persona

To get a clear overview of the target user I created a persona based on my findings to effectively represent their needs and characteristics of them. I continuously placed myself in Maya's "shoes" to empathize with her throughout the design process.

Storyboard

After iteration, I created a storyboard to visually predict and explore my users' experience with the gift giving-experience app.
