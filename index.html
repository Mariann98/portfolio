About

Hey, my name is Mariann and I am currently majoring in UX design at THUAS. One of the things I love most about UX design is prototyping, I can get lost in time when I begin with it. If there would be one thing I have to do for the rest of my life, that would be it.
I have always known that I want to work in the IT field but I never knew in what role, till I found out about UX and UI design which sparked my interest immediately. Since the beginning of 2020, I started to develop my UI skills by creating different apps that would benefit my family and myself. You can read more about them in my projects.
During my studies in the university, I have developed my skills in research and programming which has given me a new perspective on UX design.
