About me

I am a first-year student at The Hague University of Applied Sciences, majoring in UX Design. I come from a small country by the Baltic sea - Estonia, which is known for being digitally advanced.

I have always known that I want to work in the IT field but I never knew in what role, till I found out about UX and UI design which sparked my interest immediately. Since the beginning of 2020, I started to develop my UI skills by creating different apps that would benefit my family and myself. You can read more about them in my portfolio.

During my studies in the university, I have developed my skills in research and programming which has given me a new perspective on UX design.
